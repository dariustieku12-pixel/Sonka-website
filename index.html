How Sonka works

No booking system. No in-app payment. Just connect with drivers and get things moving.

01

Open the map

Gold dots show available drivers near you in real-time. Filter by truck, tricycle or cargo bike.

02

View driver profiles

See vehicle details, ratings, completed trips and availability status before you contact anyone.

03

Call or message

Tap the driver's profile and call or message directly. Discuss your goods, route and agree a price.

04

Pay your way

Pay cash or MoMo directly to the driver. Sonka never touches your money.
